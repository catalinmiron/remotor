Remotor - Remote Python (and more) jobs

This site is a work in progress so apologies in advance for the roughness around the edges.

I firmly believe that the future of work is remote. Personally I'm based in the Wild West of Spain (Extremadura) where there are very few job opportunities, but manage to get by on remote consulting work.

The general idea is to gather links to Python jobs from a whole lot of websites which carry ads for remote Python jobs. It will likely expand to include other languages, and perhaps other types of remote work opportunities which aren't tech-related.

This site is run on a totally voluntary basis and we aren't taking any kind of a cut or kickback from the job sites.
